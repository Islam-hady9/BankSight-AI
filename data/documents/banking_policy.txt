BANKING POLICIES AND PROCEDURES

==================================
1. ACCOUNT OPENING REQUIREMENTS
==================================

Personal Checking Account:
- Valid government-issued photo ID (Driver's license, Passport)
- Proof of address (Utility bill, Lease agreement - must be within 90 days)
- Social Security Number or Tax ID
- Minimum opening deposit: $100
- Must be 18 years or older

Personal Savings Account:
- Valid government-issued photo ID
- Proof of address
- Social Security Number
- Minimum opening deposit: $500
- Must be 18 years or older
- Maximum of 6 withdrawals per month (Regulation D)

Student Checking Account:
- Valid student ID
- Proof of enrollment
- Minimum opening deposit: $25
- No monthly maintenance fee for students
- Must be between 16-25 years old

==================================
2. FEE SCHEDULE
==================================

Monthly Maintenance Fees:
- Checking Account: $12/month (waived with $1,500 minimum balance)
- Savings Account: $5/month (waived with $500 minimum balance)
- Student Checking: $0
- Premium Checking: $25/month (includes premium benefits)

Transaction Fees:
- Domestic Wire Transfer: $25 per transaction
- International Wire Transfer: $45 per transaction
- Stop Payment: $35 per request
- Overdraft Fee: $35 per occurrence
- Returned Item Fee: $35 per item
- ATM Fee (out-of-network): $3.00 per transaction

Paper Statement Fee:
- $5 per month (waived if enrolled in e-statements)

==================================
3. OVERDRAFT PROTECTION
==================================

Overdraft Protection Service:
- Available for checking accounts
- Links to savings account or credit card
- Transfer fee: $10 per transfer
- Automatic transfer if checking balance goes negative
- Protection up to available balance in linked account

Overdraft Coverage:
- Covers checks, ACH, and recurring payments
- Does NOT cover ATM withdrawals or one-time debit card purchases unless opted in
- Fee: $35 per occurrence
- Maximum 3 fees per day

==================================
4. ATM AND DEBIT CARD SERVICES
==================================

Daily Limits:
- ATM Withdrawal: $500 (Standard), $1,000 (Premium)
- Debit Card Purchase: $2,500 (Standard), $5,000 (Premium)
- Can be temporarily increased by calling customer service

Card Replacement:
- Standard delivery (7-10 business days): Free
- Expedited delivery (2-3 business days): $25
- Overnight delivery: $50

Lost or Stolen Card:
- Report immediately to avoid liability
- No liability if reported before unauthorized use
- Maximum liability: $50 if reported within 2 business days
- Maximum liability: $500 if reported within 60 days

==================================
5. WIRE TRANSFERS
==================================

Domestic Wire Transfer:
- Same-day processing if initiated before 3 PM ET
- Fee: $25
- Required information: Recipient name, bank routing number, account number

International Wire Transfer:
- Processing time: 1-3 business days
- Fee: $45
- Required information: SWIFT code, IBAN, recipient bank details
- Currency conversion fees may apply

Wire Transfer Limits:
- Online: $5,000 per day
- In-branch or by phone: $25,000 per day
- Can be increased with verification

==================================
6. MOBILE AND ONLINE BANKING
==================================

Features:
- 24/7 account access
- Bill pay service (free)
- Mobile check deposit (free, up to $5,000 per day)
- Account alerts (email and SMS)
- Person-to-person payments (Zelle)
- Budgeting tools

Security:
- Two-factor authentication required
- Biometric login available (fingerprint, face recognition)
- Automatic timeout after 10 minutes of inactivity
- Real-time fraud monitoring

==================================
7. INTEREST RATES
==================================

Savings Account APY:
- Balance $0 - $999: 0.01%
- Balance $1,000 - $9,999: 0.05%
- Balance $10,000+: 0.10%

Money Market Account APY:
- Balance $2,500 - $24,999: 0.15%
- Balance $25,000 - $99,999: 0.25%
- Balance $100,000+: 0.35%

Certificate of Deposit (CD) APY:
- 6 months: 0.50%
- 1 year: 1.00%
- 2 years: 1.50%
- 5 years: 2.00%
- Early withdrawal penalty applies

==================================
8. DISPUTE RESOLUTION
==================================

Unauthorized Transaction Dispute:
- Report within 60 days of statement date
- Temporary credit issued within 10 business days
- Investigation completed within 45 days (90 days for new accounts)

Merchant Dispute (Chargeback):
- Must attempt to resolve with merchant first
- File dispute within 60 days of transaction
- Provide documentation supporting dispute
- Investigation completed within 30-60 days

==================================
9. ACCOUNT CLOSURE
==================================

Voluntary Closure:
- Can be done online, by phone, or in-branch
- No closure fee if account open for 6+ months
- Early closure fee: $25 (if closed within 6 months)
- Outstanding checks may cause issues - wait for clearance
- Direct deposits should be redirected before closure

Involuntary Closure:
- Bank may close account for excessive overdrafts
- Bank may close account for fraudulent activity
- Notice provided 30 days before closure (unless fraud)
